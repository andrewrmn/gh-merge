Git Merge is a one-day conference dedicated to the version control tool that started it all—and the people who use it every day. From technical sessions to hands-on workshops, developers of all experience levels will find new ways to use, build on, and scale Git on their teams.

How to get to Git Merge

Travel tips for every leg of your trip.

Venue

Git Merge will be hosted in the Convent dels Àngels at MACBA, the historic chapel adjacent to the Museum of Contemporary Art in Barcelona.

Travel and transportation

We recommend flying into Barcelona—El Prat International Airport (BCN).

The Convent dels Àngels is accessible via the Barcelona Metro. You can take the red or green lines to the Catalunya station and walk a few short blocks to the venue.

Hotels

You’ll find a variety of hotels and AirBnBs near the venue.
